For Attendees

We're a group of non-traditional Software Engineers, data analysts, dropouts, and career-switchers. Our goal is to be the trusted source of what's happening in the Software world.

You should expect: amazing, friendly, and developers of ALL levels that form the backbone of the New York City tech.

For Sponsors & Partners

We have an growing member base at 200 people a month. We're promoted through the Meetup.com algorithm and are the #1 meetup in the tech category and continue to do so through a during and after a pandemic. Our principle-based operations keep our organization providing highest possible value for each attendee, building trust and friendships with the kingmakers of the 21st century, Software Engineers.

Our Principles include: Genuine and Honest connections over superficial networkers, Inclusion of people at ANY level over specific technologies and familiarity, Agile and Consistent over Slow and Unpredictable
