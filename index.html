New York Code & Coffee

An informal meetup group for coders of ALL levels by developers of all levels. Please bear with us as we develop a cooler looking website in Next.js.

The Meetup: https://www.meetup.com/new-york-code-coffee/

Official NYC Code & Coffee community Discord: https://discord.com/invite/xZNGCz6uXg

By attending our events you agree to the Code of Conduct

Our principles:

  • Genuine and Honest connections
  • Inclusion of all people at ANY level in tech rather than specification to specific technologies or familiarity
  • Agile and Consistent over Slow and Unpredictable
