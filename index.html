You should expect: Friendly folks, scholars of Software, job refferals, really cool side projects, and oblige by the Code of Conduct

Our Principles

Genuine connection

We strive for real in-person connections and capitalize on that 3 hour afternoon window every 2 Sundays!

We are always greatful for feedback and LOVE to socialize in-person. Feel free to message us ahead of time with questions or concerns!

Open Inclusion

Unlike other meetups, you don't need to know programming with XYZ.js, web3, or even a specific programming language. People code in everything!

After the 2:30 intro circle(standup) you'll figure out who's who and strike up a conversation or go to your laptop. It's fully up to you!

Agile Consistency

We adapt fast to fit the needs of our members. As we aim for consistent events, we also use qualitative feedback to improve time over time.

Since 2017, we've experimented and tweaked that same playbook that worked in DC to create(as of 2021) the fastest growing meetup in NYC!

Just bring a laptop

All you need to bring is a laptop. We are VERY newbie friendly, social, and openly postiive developers interested in meeting more developers.

We provide the space and the other cool people

Community First

We are students, career switchers, FANG engineers, dropouts, early startup devs, bootcamp grads, and intersectional tech folk of all ages.

On-top of our current bi-weekly C&C event - we plan on hosting: job fairs, HHs, and hackathons

Independently owned

Not funded by a Big Tech or Venture Capital, we aim to align our brand to those that reflect our community values and vision.

Our organizers are volunteers that actively seek to make your experience as valuable as possible
