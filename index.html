We are New York Code & Coffee. A meetup group for Software folks of all levels by developers of all levels. Please bear with us as we attempt to open source the website.

You can reach us here: https://www.meetup.com/new-york-code-coffee/

The Official NYC Code & Coffee community Discord: https://discord.com/invite/xZNGCz6uXg

Our principles:

  • Genuine person-to-person connection
  • Inclusion of all people of ANY level in tech rather than specification to one technology
  • Agile and Consistent over Slow and Unpredictable
